# HR_Analytics_Insights
Analyzed data from 1,400+ employees to identify the root causes of a 16.09% attrition rate. Identified key turnover drivers—primarily the 26-35 age group and Sales roles—linked to salary and education. Transformed raw data into a strategic roadmap to improve talent retention and organizational stability.
| Category | Finding | Insight |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Overall Stability | 16.09% Attrition | A critical indicator for assessing workforce sustainability. |
| Demographic Trend | Age Group 26-35 | 116 staff exits, impacting mid-level talent pools. |
| Financial Factor | Salary 5K or less | 163 departures point to a compensation concern. |
| At-Risk Roles | Sales and Research | Sales Reps lead at 39.29% turnover rate. |
| Academic Background | Life Sciences / Medical | These sectors show the highest turnover rates. |
